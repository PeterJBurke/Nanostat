Instructions:

Please click on button to run a sweep. Blue LED on NanoStat will flash during sweep. At end of sweep, file can be downloaded below.

Sweep modes:

CV = Cyclic voltammetry.
NPV = Normal pulsed voltammetry.
SWV = Square wave voltammetry.
CA = Chronoamperometry.
DC = Fixed DC bias.
IV = IV curve.
Cal = calibrate ESP32Stat.
